# Route Planning Notes

## Košice → Vienna Route

**Distance:** 313 mi (504 km)
**Drive Time:** 4 hrs 48 mins

### Key Stops:
1. **Castle of Sárospatak** (Hungary) - 48.3167, 21.5667
   - Restored castle with museum of 17th-century noble life
   
2. **Hímesudvar Winery** (Tokaj, Hungary) - 48.1167, 21.4167
   - Family-owned winery with wine tasting

3. **Tokaj Wine Region** (Hungary) - 48.1167, 21.4167
   - UNESCO World Heritage wine region

4. **Boldogkő Castle** (Hungary) - 48.3833, 21.1500
   - Medieval fortress with panoramic views

5. **Eger** (Hungary) - 47.9026, 20.3770
   - Historic city with Eger Castle, minarets, thermal baths

6. **Budapest** (Hungary) - 47.4979, 19.0402
   - Major city stop (optional detour)

7. **Bratislava** (Slovakia) - 48.1486, 17.1077
   - Capital city with castle and old town
